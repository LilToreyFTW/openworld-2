Doom Patrol Mission

Intro — First deployment

Briefing

You are cleared for deployment. Your first assignment: reach the Doom Patrol Tower and link up with the gathering area. The Tower is the main hub for operations across all seven planets.

From there you can accept land and space quests, run missions, join multiplayer (TDM, Domination, CTF, Search and Destroy), survive Zombies, and unlock 50 weapons with 10 prestige camos each.

Objective: Enter the Doom Patrol Tower (Hub) and report in.
